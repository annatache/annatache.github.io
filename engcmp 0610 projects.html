VIDEO PROJECT

My video project was inspired by the concept of "mean world syndrome," a cognitive bias that the world is more dangerous than it actually is, formed by constant exposure to violence-related content. I wanted to create a video, using clips from moments in my life, that combats that idea, emphasizing the beauty of every little moment in life. I want this video to serve as a reminder to myself and others that there is so much joy to be found in life, even in the smallest of moments.
